A control that transforms standard Power Apps choice fields into visually rich dropdowns with advanced customization options.
The Advanced Dropdown Component supports the vast majority of Microsoft's Segoe UI Symbol Font icons, color coding with three intensity levels, flexible sizing, and smart sorting capabilities.
It features custom icon support with 1,800+ available icons, color customization for icons/borders/backgrounds, component height options, and graceful fallbacks - making it perfect for status indicators, priority selectors, category selection, or any scenario requiring visually enhanced choice fields.
