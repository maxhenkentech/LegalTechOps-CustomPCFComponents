A control that brings interactive risk assessment directly into your Power Apps.
The Risk Matrix Component lets you plot and visualize risks on configurable 2x2, 3x3, or 4x4 grids with customizable axis labels, colors, and size options.
It features dynamic risk labeling (LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, CRITICAL), responsive design, and modern Fluent UI styling - making it perfect for legal, compliance, and project risk evaluations, or for any other scenario where you need a matrix.
